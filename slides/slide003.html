Main ML Challenges

Challenge 1: Bad Data

Insufficient Quantity

ML needs thousands of examples. "The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Data."

Nonrepresentative Data

Sampling bias (e.g., "Dewey Defeats Truman"). Data must reflect the real world.

Poor Quality & Irrelevant Features

Errors, outliers, and noise. Garbage In, Garbage Out.

Challenge 2: Bad Algorithms

Overfitting

Model learns the noise, not the signal. Performs well on train, poor on test.

High Variance
Underfitting

Model is too simple to capture the underlying structure.

High Bias

"The goal is to generalize to new instances, not just memorize training data."

Mitigation Strategies

Symptom Likely Cause Fix / Mitigation
High Variance
(Overfitting)		 Model is too complex; too many parameters relative to data.
  • Get more training data
  • Simplify model
  • Regularization
High Bias
(Underfitting)		 Model is too simple; assumptions too strong.
  • More complex model
  • Better features
  • Reduce regularization
